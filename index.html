FlipList Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

FlipList ("we", "our", or "the app") is a music quiz game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

FlipList collects minimal data to provide and improve the app experience:

  • Game scores: Your high scores are stored locally on your device only.
  • Usage analytics: We collect anonymous usage data such as which themes are played and how often, to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Device sensors: The app uses your device's accelerometer to detect when you flip your phone. This data is processed locally and never transmitted.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Payment information (handled by Google Play)

Third-Party Services

FlipList uses Deezer to stream music previews. When you play a song, your device connects to Deezer's servers to retrieve the audio. Deezer's privacy policy applies to their services: https://www.deezer.com/legal/personal-datas

Data Storage

All game data (scores, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain external servers that store your personal data.

Children's Privacy

FlipList is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
