FlipList Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best gaming experience, FlipList collects and stores the following information:

  • Game Progress & Profile: We store your chosen username and your in-game progress (including levels completed, coin balance, and active power-ups). If you play as a Guest, this data is stored locally on your device.
  • Google Sign-In & Cloud Saves: If you choose to log in with Google to back up your progress, we collect your basic Google profile information (such as your Google display name, email address, and a unique authentication ID) to securely link your game data to your account.
  • Analytics & Crash Reporting: We use tools like Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the app performs and to fix bugs. This data is anonymized.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers and personalized data to show you relevant ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save, sync, and restore your in-game progress across devices.
  • To personalize your profile (e.g., displaying your chosen username in-game).
  • To provide necessary customer support.
  • To serve advertisements which keep the game free to play.
  • To improve game mechanics based on crash reports and analytics.

3. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. If you have logged in using Google and wish to delete your account and associated cloud save data, you can do so by contacting us at the email address below, or by disconnecting the FlipList app from your Google Account settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our game relies on services provided by Google (Google Play Services, AdMob, Firebase). We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children's Privacy

FlipList does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
